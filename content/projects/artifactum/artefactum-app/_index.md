# The Artefactum App: The AI Engine Behind the Mystery

Every story needs a storyteller. For the Artifactum Mystery Crimes series, that storyteller is an app.

This isn't just a game; it's a platform. The Artefactum App is the AI-powered engine I built to generate, manage, and run the murder mystery experiences. Think of it as a "Mystery in a Box" toolkit, but the box is powered by advanced AI and a whole lot of Python.

The app is the central nervous system for each mystery. It's responsible for:

- **Character Generation:** Crafting unique characters with backstories, secrets, and motivations.
- **Clue Management:** Distributing clues to the right characters at the right time.
- **Plot Weaving:** Weaving a tangled web of relationships and events that leads to the final reveal.
- **Real-time Interaction:** (Coming Soon) Allowing players to interact with the game world and non-player characters through a simple interface.
